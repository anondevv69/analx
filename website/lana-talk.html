  3. Ask follow-ups in plain English in the same chat; use /reset to clear context.

Groups: add @AnalAgent_bot as a member. Use /q or include a Solana address in your message. If the bot doesn’t see plain messages, mention @AnalAgent_bot or ask the group admin to turn off Group Privacy for the bot in BotFather.
